Cold water shake

Feeling thirsty on a scorching hot day but cant find any of the ingredients to make a nice tasty banana or some other sort of smoothie? Worry not, this recipe will come in clutch

Ingredients

  • Tap water
  • Cubes of ice
  • A large glass

Instructions

  1. Pour the water into your cup
  2. Put a bunch of ice cubes in the water
  3. put all of it in the blender
  4. Now you can enjoy your drink! cold and refreshing! It also doesn't have any calories, so you can drink away as much as you want.
